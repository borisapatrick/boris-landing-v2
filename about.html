ABOUT BORIS ENTERPRISES

OUR STORY

Boris Enterprises was built on a simple idea: fix cars right, charge fair prices, and treat people like neighbors — not numbers. Based in East Jordan, Michigan, we've been turning wrenches and building trust in Charlevoix County for over two decades.

We're not a chain. We're not a dealership service center. We're a locally owned Northern Michigan shop where the guy who diagnoses your problem is the same guy who fixes it. That's how it should be.

Whether you drive a daily commuter, a work truck, or the family minivan — we work on all makes and models. If it's got an engine, we can fix it.
