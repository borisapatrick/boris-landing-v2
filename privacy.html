PRIVACY POLICY

Last updated: February 21, 2026

Boris Enterprises ("we," "us," or "our") operates the website borisenterprises.com and provides auto repair services in East Jordan, Michigan. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices regarding your data.

Information We Collect

When you use our website or schedule a service, we may collect the following information:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Vehicle information (year, make, model, license plate)
  • Appointment details and service requests
  • Account information when you sign in through Google

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Schedule and manage repair appointments
  • Communicate with you about your vehicle service
  • Send service updates, appointment reminders, and follow-ups
  • Respond to your questions and requests
  • Improve our website and services

Authentication & Google Sign-In

Our website uses Firebase Authentication and Google Sign-In, services provided by Google LLC, to manage user accounts. You may sign in using your Google account. When you do, we receive your name, email address, and profile photo from Google. We do not receive or store your Google password. Your authentication session is managed by Firebase, and we use authentication tokens to keep you signed in.

Email & SMS Communications

With your consent, we may send you email and SMS messages related to appointment confirmations, service updates, and reminders. Emails are sent through SendGrid and text messages are sent through Twilio, both operated by Twilio Inc. You can opt out of non-essential communications at any time by updating your preferences in your account dashboard or by contacting us directly.

Cookies & Local Storage

Our website uses cookies and local storage primarily for Firebase authentication tokens (managed by Google LLC), which keep you signed in during your session. We do not use advertising or third-party tracking cookies.

Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored using Cloud Firestore and other Google LLC infrastructure, which employ industry-standard security measures including encryption in transit and at rest. While we take reasonable steps to protect your information, no method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

Third-Party Services

We rely on the following third-party service providers to operate our website and communicate with you. These companies act as service providers on behalf of Boris Enterprises and only process your data as needed to deliver their services to us.

Google LLC

  • Firebase Authentication & Google Sign-In — lets you create an account and sign in securely, including signing in with your Google account.
  • Cloud Firestore — stores your account information, vehicle details, and service records.
  • Firebase Hosting — serves the website itself.

You can review Google's Privacy Policy for details on how Google handles data across these services.

Twilio Inc.

  • SendGrid (email) — sends appointment confirmations, service updates, and other email communications on our behalf.
  • Twilio (SMS) — sends text-message reminders and notifications about your appointments and services.

You can review Twilio's Privacy Policy for details on how Twilio and SendGrid handle data.

Data Retention & Deletion

We retain your personal information only as long as necessary to provide our services and fulfill the purposes described in this policy. You may request deletion of your account and personal data at any time by contacting us at contact@borisenterprises.com or calling 231-675-0723. We will process deletion requests within a reasonable timeframe.

Children's Privacy

Our website and services are not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will take steps to remove that information.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
