Diagnostics & Inspections

We'll tell you exactly what's wrong — no guessing. If your check engine light is on or something just doesn't feel right, our diagnostic tools and experience will pinpoint the problem so you only pay for what actually needs fixing.

Brake Service

Your safety is our priority — we get your brakes right. Squealing, grinding, or a soft brake pedal are signs you shouldn't ignore. Stop by our East Jordan shop for a brake inspection before a small issue becomes a big one.

Transmission

Manual or automatic — we keep your transmission shifting smooth. Hard shifts, slipping gears, or delayed engagement are common signs of transmission trouble. We service all makes and models at our shop in East Jordan.
